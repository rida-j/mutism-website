The Selective Mutism Foundation (SMF) is a registered nonprofit organization with the mission to (1) collaborate on education, awareness and treatment for Selective Mutism; and (2) enable individuals to find their voice and overcome barriers.

The vision of the foundation is to be a respected leader in awareness, education and treatment for individuals who affected by Selected Mutism; and to provide a common ground where people with SM from all over the world can network and support one another.

Since SMF is a nonprofit organization (not a charity), we are not eligible for many, if not most, funding opportunities. Hence, your donation will assist us to carry on our mission, and continue to make positive impacts for those impacted by selective mutism.

Any amount is greatly appreciated!

SMF is proud to present VOCAL. This book may change your child’s life in the matter of hours!

In a peer-reviewed published studies, Dr. Poling Bork demonstrated how she successfully guided parents and educators to help their children overcome selective mutism within 130 minutes each (within 8 sessions). This is remarkable, especially since these children had long-standing SM and had received numerous failed interventions before Dr. Bork’s involvement. Due to this success, Dr. Bork is detailing the entire process in her book "VOCAL: Help Your Child Conquer Selective Mutism in <3 Hours with a Proven Strategy," so that everyone can access this easy-to-understand information, which tends to be reserved for the research world.

Within 2 years, VOCAL has touched SM communities worldwide and has been translated into 8 languages.

Dr. Bork herself, as well as all three of her children, have suffered from SM, so she has made it a personal mission to assist others impacted by it.
